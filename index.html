Where to stay in Kaunas

Keep in mind that the wedding night we will all sleep at the venue, which is around 45 min away from Kaunas

For the rest of the days, in case you want to spend some days in the city and/or explore the country, here are some places we can recommend depending on your budget:

  • Hotel 1 - 90€ / night
  • Hotel 2 - 80€ / night
  • AirBnB in the Old Town - 30-40€ / night
  • AirBnB in the outskirts of the city - 15-20€ / night
  • Hostel - 15€ / night
